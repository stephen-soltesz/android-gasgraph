How-to...

View Graphs

Hold your phone in landscape mode to turn your data into beautiful graphs. Swipe left and right to see all your graphs.

Add a new Fill-up

Swipe left to the History panel. Press the plus button at the bottom of the screen.

Edit an existing Fill-up

Press and hold on the entry you want to edit. This will take you to the edit record screen.

Entering A Number

To enter a number, touch the dial you want to adjust, then without lifting your finger, drag down until your number is selected. This will give you the most control with the least effort.

Share Graphs

To share a graph, press and hold the image you want to share. This will load a list of applications for you to choose from.
